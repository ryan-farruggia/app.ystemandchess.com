Why Chess?

Promotes Brain Growth

Chess helps exercise both sides of the brain, greatly improves memory, raises IQ and test scores, and can even prevent Alzheimer's.

Enhances Problem Solving

A highly sought after STEM skill is mastery in problem solving. Chess teaches proper planning, foresight, pattern recognition, and the role of consequences.

Recovery

Greatly promotes healthy brain growth when recovering from stroke or a disability.

Creativity

Many career paths in STEM require two critical skillsets: critical thinking and creativity. Many situations require us to have a strong bond between the creative and problem solving sides of our brains.
